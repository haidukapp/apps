Gym Log

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2025

Overview

Gym Log is a workout tracking application. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles your information. The short version: we collect nothing. All data stays on your device.

Data We Collect

Gym Log does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account or registration is required
  • No internet connection is used
  • No analytics or crash reporting tools are integrated
  • No advertising SDKs are included
  • No data is sent to any server

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Workout programs and exercises you create or modify
  • Workout history and statistics
  • App settings (theme, language)
  • Optional profile name and bio (entered by you)

This data never leaves your device. It is stored using the device's local storage and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Notifications

The app uses local notifications for the rest timer and workout reminders. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any external servers or services.

Third-Party Services

Gym Log does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect data.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this Privacy Policy, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date.
